Sauce Bolognese

Short Description:
The original italian spaghetti sauce.

Ingredient:
Tomato	1 kilogram
Vegetable broth	100 milliliter
Onion (white)	1 piece
Minced meat	300 gram

Instructions:
Cut the tomatoes and the onion into little pieces. First fry the onion and the meat, then add the vegetable broth and cook for 15 minutes. Then add the tomatoes and cook for another 45 minutes. Season with some herb salt and pepper.

Difficulty: challenging

Duration: 60 minutes
